🔌 How this app works
This app does not automate anything on your phone directly — Android doesn't allow apps to silently type into other apps in the background. Instead, this app talks to your own server (the one you deployed on Railway/Render/etc.), which does the actual work 24/7. That's why it works even if your phone is off.
