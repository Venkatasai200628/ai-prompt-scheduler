Phone Mode via Kiwi Browser
1
Install Kiwi Browser on your Android phone — it's a Chrome-based browser that supports extensions
2
Open Kiwi → type chrome://extensions → turn on Developer Mode
3
Click Load Unpacked → select the extension folder from your phone (transfer via USB or Google Drive)
4
The extension works exactly the same — schedule prompts on your phone and they fire automatically even when your laptop is off
